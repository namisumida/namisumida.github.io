About me

I am a data visualization designer and developer. This site is home to some of my personal and professional work.

I am currently a data visualization developer at the San Francisco Chronicle.

SELECTED WORK

  • These five maps show how California is divided between Democrats and Republicans

    Five different ways to map California's registered voters

  • What are the widest and narrowest residential streets in San Francisco?

    An analysis and research comparing street widths of all S.F. residential streets and the value of land below them to understand the tradeoffs between using land for streets vs. other urban purposes.

  • These big banks were the least likely to serve Bay Area communities of color with PPP loans

    An analysis of data on PPP loans shows some lenders processing more PPP loans to businesses in higher-income, predominantly white area, compared to areas that are lower-income or have a higher percentage of Black and Hispanic residents.

  • Traffic congestion is making a comeback in the Bay Area, but in a strange new way

    An analysis of traffic congestion data shows that weekend traffic is making a comeback faster than weekday traffic.

  • Bay Area traffic is now back in full force. Here's a look at the data

    An analysis of hourly bridge toll data shows traffic on some Bay Area bridges is returning to pre-pandemic levels.

  • Parking meter use shows the unevenness of S.F.'s pandemic recovery

    An analysis of parking meter transactions show activity in residential areas is close to pre-pandemic levels, but traffic to downtown is still low.

  • How well do you understand COVID-19 rates in your community?

    An interactive quiz that tests readers' knowledge of COVID-19 case rates in their area.

  • Cell phone data shows Bay Area remains extreme when it comes to staying at home during pandemic

    An analysis of cell phone data to understand Bay Area residents' activity during the pandemic.

  • How biotech and pharma companies pay their CEOs, and their workers

    An analysis of CEO compensation for 230 biotech and pharma companies. Read more about the process of making this story here.

  • The first coronavirus vaccines have arrived. Here's where the rest stand.

    A close look at some of the most promising coronavirus vaccines and their estimated timelines for research and production.

  • A record number of biotechs are going public. Here's how they're performing.

  • A timeline of how the pandemic hit retail

    Data visualizations that show trends in the retail industry, such as store closures, reopenings and layoffs/furloughs, during the coronavirus pandemic.
