About me

I am a data journalist at the San Francisco Chronicle covering local politics, elections and education — and occassionally personal interests like books, hiking and trees.

Previously, I worked as a newsroom developer at Industry Dive and a research analyst at the Pew Research Center’s Journalism & Media group.
