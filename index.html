About me

I am a data analyst who likes to tell stories through interactive graphics. This site is home to all of my personal data visualizations, along with reports that I've written professionally.

Currently, I am working as a Research Analyst at the Pew Research Center's Journalism & Media project, doing social science research on journalism and the news media.
