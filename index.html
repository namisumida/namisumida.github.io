I am a data visualization developer and designer. This site is home to my personal and professional work.

Currently, I am working as a Research Analyst at the Pew Research Center's Journalism & Media project, conducting social science research on journalism and the news media.

Data Visualizations

  • Standards of Living

    Using data from the World Bank, I compared several health metrics in the US and some of the least developed countries in the world to better understand how living standards can widely vary around the world.

  • Paths to the House of Representatives

    A look at past experiences of the members of the House of Representatives, including their education, prior careers, and previously-held political offices.

  • League of Legends visualizations
